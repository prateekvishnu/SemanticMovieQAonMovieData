Towards the end of colonial India, Shahid Khan impersonates the legendary Sultana Daku in order to rob British trains. When Sultana discovers Khan's actions, Khan exiles Khan from the village. As a result, Shahid becomes a henchman for coalmine owner Ramadhir Singh. Shahid has ambitions to takeover the coalmine, which angers Ramadhir. Shahid is consequently killed. Shahid's son, Sardar, is deeply angered and seeks vengeance for the death of Sardar's father at the hands of Ramadhir. Sardar makes vengeance Sardar's mission to avenge Sardar's father. Ramadhir becomes a powerful politician. Sardar becomes a fearless gang lord.
